## Time-boxed observations on the MCP

- Tabular API is the workhorse — but coverage is patchy. About a third of the datasets I touched today had usable Tabular API resources. The rest were either off-platform redirects (INSEE on insee.fr, IRDES on ecosante.fr) or too-large national files (SIRENE).
- Region-level republishings (like Région IDF re-publishing INSEE BPE) are often the only way to get commune-level data at queryable size. Other regional aggregators worth looking at: Région Bretagne, Région Pays de la Loire, Région Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes — all showed up in searches today.
- For aggregate counts across thousands of rows, the Tabular API's max page_size of 200 still means many round-trips. Often more efficient to grab the underlying CSV URL once and aggregate locally during Task 6.
